Let \(\pqty{\Omega, \mathcal{F}, P}\) be a probability space, let \(\mathcal{H}\) be a sub-\(\sigma\)-algebra, then we can define the \key{conditional expectation} a.s. as a random variable \(Y\) such that:
\begin{equation*}
    E(YH) = E\pqty{XH} \quad \forall H \in \mathcal{H}
\end{equation*}
